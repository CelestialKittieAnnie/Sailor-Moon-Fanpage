Sailor Moon

Sailor Moon, also known as Usagi Tsukino, is the main protagonist of the series. She is the Sailor Guardian of love and justice, and she fights to protect Earth from evil forces. Usagi is originally from Earth and the Moon Kingdom.

Sailor Mercury

Sailor Mercury, also known as Ami Mizuno, is the Sailor Guardian of water and intelligence. She uses her intelligence and water-based powers to aid the Sailor Guardians. Ami is originally from Earth and the planet Mercury.

Sailor Mars

Sailor Mars, also known as Rei Hino, is the Sailor Guardian of fire and passion. She uses her fire-based powers and spiritual abilities to fight against evil. Rei is originally from Earth and the planet Mars.

Sailor Jupiter

Sailor Jupiter, also known as Makoto Kino, is the Sailor Guardian of strength and protection. She uses her lightning-based powers and physical strength to protect her friends. Makoto is originally from Earth and the planet Jupiter.

Sailor Venus

Sailor Venus, also known as Minako Aino, is the Sailor Guardian of love and beauty. She uses her light-based powers and agility to fight against evil. Minako is originally from Earth and the planet Venus.
