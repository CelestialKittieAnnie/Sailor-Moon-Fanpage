Main Characters

Usagi Tsukino / Sailor Moon

Usagi Tsukino is the main protagonist of the Sailor Moon series. She is a teenage girl who transforms into the magical warrior Sailor Moon to fight against evil forces. She is known for her kind heart and strong sense of justice.

Appeared in: Shows, Movies, Mangas

Ami Mizuno / Sailor Mercury

Ami Mizuno is a highly intelligent and studious girl who transforms into Sailor Mercury. She uses her intelligence and water-based powers to aid the Sailor Guardians in their battles.

Appeared in: Shows, Movies, Mangas

Supporting Characters

Mamoru Chiba / Tuxedo Mask

Mamoru Chiba is Usagi's love interest and a key ally to the Sailor Guardians. He transforms into Tuxedo Mask and provides support to the team with his strength and wisdom.

Appeared in: Shows, Movies, Mangas
